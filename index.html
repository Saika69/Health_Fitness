About Me

Hello, I'm Saika Noor, a certified fitness professional from the K11 School of Fitness Sciences, registered by REPS India. I am also a sports nutrition coach. With expertise in fitness and nutrition, I help individuals achieve their fitness goals using evidence-based practices and personalized guidance. Whether you're looking to improve your strength, manage a health condition, or enhance your overall wellness, I'm here to help you succeed!
